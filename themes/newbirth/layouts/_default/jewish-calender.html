Sacred & Civil Months · Season · Production · Festivals · Life Years of Jesus (Padgett-Samuels)

Sacred Year

Begins with Nisan (Month 1). Biblical order of feasts (Ex 12:2). Life Year 1 of Jesus = Nisan.

Civil Year

Begins with Tishri (Month 7 sacred = Month 1 civil). Rosh Hashanah — Jewish New Year.

Life Years of Jesus

Per Padgett-Samuels: born Nisan 7 BC (LY 1), died Adar II 18 March 29 AD (end LY 12). Each month = one life year.

Lunar Months

Each month starts at the new moon (Rosh Chodesh). 29 or 30 days. Leap month Adar II added 7 times in 19 years.
